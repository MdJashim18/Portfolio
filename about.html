My full name is Mohammad Jasim Uddin. I was born in a village called Barumchada, Anwara Thana, Chittagong District, Chittagong Division, Bangladesh.I am from middle-class family. My mother's name is Laila Begum and father's name is Mohammad Syed.My father was remittance warrior.After that he was a grocery store owner.My mother was a housewife.I received my first formal education from my mother. Then I was admitted to Barumchada Primary School. There I studied up to the third grade. Then I did Hifz from the Hifz department in the Madrasa. After Hifz, I completed my studies from Chobhania Alia Kamil Madrasa to the eighth grade. Chobhania Alia Kamil Madrasa did not have a science department. So I was admitted to the ninth grade of the Science department in Jamia Ahmadia Sunnia Alia Madrasa. There I completed up to the tenth grade. Then I passed the eleventh and twelfth grades from Patiya Government College. Now I am studying in the Computer Science and Engineering department at Rangamati University of Science and Technology.
